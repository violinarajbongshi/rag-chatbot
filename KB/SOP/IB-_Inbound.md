# IB- Inbound

Source: https://sites.google.com/shiprocket.com/sop-shiprocket/call-script/ib-inbound

Back IB- Inbound Inbound:- ( English) Opening: Good morning /afternoon/evening...Welcome to Shiprocket, My Name is ................. how may I assist you ? ACKNOWLEDGEMENT REQUIRED - Surely, I will assist you regarding your concern (Mention Seller's Concern) Hold Script: May I place your call on hold for 60 seconds, s o that i can give you better information ? (system check ) Un-hold Script: Thank you for being on hold.! Thank you for being on hold, I am sorry to keep you wait for such a long time. (if hold exceeds more than sixty seconds). Further Assistance: Is there anything else I may assist you regarding shiprocket ! CSAT Script: If you are satisfied with the information provided on the call, then please share your valuable feedback with us over the email that you will receive after this call Google Review Script : If you are satisfied with the information provided on the call, then please share your valuable review on shiprocket website. You will receive the link after this call on your registered email id. Closing Scripts: Thank you for calling Shiprocket , Have a good day. -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Inbound:- ( Hindi ) Opening: Good morning /afternoon/evening...Welcome to Shiprocket, My Name is ................. how may I assist you ?
