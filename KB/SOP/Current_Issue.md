# Current Issue

Source: https://sites.google.com/shiprocket.com/sop-shiprocket/current-issue

Delhivery and Xpressbees Manpower Constraints Delhivery and Xpressbees are facing low manpower and operational constraints in select pincodes. This is leading to delays in delivery attempts and a drop in overall DEL (Delivery Exception Limit) conversion. Please cascade this information to sellers and advise them to allocate shipments to other courier partners like Ecom, Amazon, and Bluedart for the affected pincodes. A communication with the list of pincodes has also been posted in the Seller Notification Panel, Please copy & paste in new TAB to check the impacted pincodes https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1WpFHZFUOOQ0kk5MXHfSsORMYkTnNdd4xli2WkH5Wov0/edit?gid=1406171577#gid=1406171577
