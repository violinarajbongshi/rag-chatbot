Back Buyer Call Handling-Inbound If a buyer is asking for shipment status and EDD then we can share the same and for any refund ,replacement or complaint/expedition, we will request buyer to connect with seller or to website from where order has been purchased. Speak For Advisor: " Sir/Ma’am, you’ve reached the seller support helpline and we provide assistance to our sellers with their operational concerns only .We will not be able to assist you for any query related to the product you have ordered. Request you to kindly contact the seller from whom you purchased the product for further help" NOTE: Do not share Seller phone number with buyer Ticket Tagging for Buyer Calls.
