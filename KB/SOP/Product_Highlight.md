Updated the text on the Seller Panel in the Label Preferences settings We have now updated the information in Label settings for India Post courier that even if the setting to hide the Shipper’s address is disabled, for this courier, the address will still be displayed. 6. Implementation of mySR signup widget on the Shiprocket Page If the buyer visits the Shiprocket website, which is possible due to the redirection URL in SMS or email, we assume that the buyer might be looking to track the order, hence, we have built an easy access button for them to track their order via Mobile No., AWB, Order Id. 7. New Status codes creation We have added 2 new status codes in the system for handling OTP-verified pickup and delivery cancellations to help segregate the status as cancelled from the rest of the statuses. Pickup Refused by Seller - OTP verified (Forward) Pickup Refused by Buyer - OTP verified (Reverse)
