# OTP Based Delivery

Source: https://sites.google.com/shiprocket.com/sop-shiprocket/otp-based-delivery

Back OTP Based Delivery There is an update on OTP (One-Time Password) delivery options for various shipment types: FWD & RTO Shipments:- Ecom now offers OTP delivery for both Forward Delivery (FWD) and Return to Origin (RTO) shipments. Prepaid Shipments:- OTP verified delivery is available for prepaid shipments with Xpressbees, Delhivery, Bluedart, and Amazon (shipment value exceeding Rs. 500 and only for Forward Delivery). OTP Activation & Deactivation: Shiprocket currently accepts requests for OTP-based delivery activation and deactivation for Xpressbees, Delhivery, and Ecom. Advisors should follow these steps: Raise an FCR Ticket Group: "Client Support" Agent: "Ankit Singh" . Subject line: OTP activation: Include "OTP activation" for activating OTP delivery. OTP deactivation: Include "OTP deactivation" for deactivating OTP delivery. Process for Team Filtering Cases from Nugget Dump: Click on seller details in ICRM. ICRM - Internal ICRM - Select (OTP Base Delhivery) Download a sample file. Sample file will be downloaded by name"otp_delivery_enable" Add: Company ID under "Company_ID" column. "0" for deactivation and "1" for activation under the "Status" column. Additional Information: Maximum wait time for successful OTP activation/deactivation is 10 minutes.
