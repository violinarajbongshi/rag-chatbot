Source: https://sites.google.com/shiprocket.com/sop-shiprocket/live/festive-season-surcharge

Festive season surcharge As part of our festive season preparedness, we have introduced a temporary surcharge across plans and courier types to manage the surge in volumes and backend cost escalations. Attaching the FAQ's to address seller queries Surcharge Structure:(Exclusive GST) Lite - ₹3 per shipment Basic to Diamond - ₹2 per shipment Effective Period: 12th September 2025 – 12th December 2025 This adjustment is necessary to offset courier partner hikes and additional operational investments we are making to ensure smooth and timely deliveries during the festive peak.
