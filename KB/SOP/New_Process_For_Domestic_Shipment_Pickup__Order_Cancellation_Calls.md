# New Process For Domestic Shipment Pickup / Order Cancellation Calls

Source: https://sites.google.com/shiprocket.com/sop-shiprocket/new-process-for-domestic-shipment-pickup-order-cancellation-calls

New Process For Domestic Shipment Pickup / Order Cancellation Calls 1. Call Transfer (10:00 AM – 6:45 PM) If a BPOC/COG agent receives any call related to Domestic Shipment Pickup or Order Cancellation , the call must be transferred to the queue: 👉 “Domestic Pickup – Transfer IH CC” This queue is live and must be used only during 10:00 AM to 6:45 PM . No exceptions are allowed during this time window. 2. Skill Alignment (Outside Core Hours) From 8:00 AM to 10:00 AM and 7:00 PM to 10:00 PM : Advisors must be aligned on the skill and need to handle cases on their own, no transfer to que. 👉 “pick , Hindi Pickup” Do NOT use the “pick , Hindi Pickup” skill between 10:00 AM and 7:00 PM . 3. Skill Restriction The skill “ Domestic Pickup – Transfer IH CC” must NOT be assigned to any BPOC/COG advisor under any circumstances. Script to transfer the call “I’m transferring your call to our concerned team for further assistance related to Pickup/order cancellation. Please stay on the call.” Click on transfer: Select the Queue : “Domestic Pickup – Transfer IH CC” Select Blind and then transfer to the queue.
